# Debt-to-Income (DTI) Ratio Guidelines
DTI is calculated by dividing total monthly debt obligations by gross monthly income.
- **Conventional**: Generally capped at 43% back-end, though some automated underwriting systems allow up to 50% with compensating factors.
- **FHA**: Max 43% standard; up to 56.9% with high credit scores and cash reserves.
- **VA**: Standard benchmark is 41%.
