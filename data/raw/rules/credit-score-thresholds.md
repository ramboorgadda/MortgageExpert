# Credit Score and Pricing Impact
The borrower's FICO score directly impacts the interest rate (LLPA - Loan Level Price Adjustments).
- **Excellent (740+)**: Best interest rates and lowest PMI costs.
- **Good (680-739)**: Standard pricing; moderate PMI premiums.
- **Fair (620-679)**: Higher interest rates; often better suited for FHA products.
- **Subprime (<620)**: Typically restricted to FHA or Non-QM (Non-Qualified Mortgage) products.
