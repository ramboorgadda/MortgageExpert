# Conventional 30-Year Fixed Rate Mortgage
The 30-year fixed-rate mortgage is the most popular loan product. It offers a stable monthly payment consisting of principal and interest that never changes over the life of the loan. 
- **Best For**: Borrowers with stable income and good credit who plan to stay in their home long-term.
- **PMI**: Required if the down payment is less than 20%. PMI can be cancelled once 20% equity is reached.""",
