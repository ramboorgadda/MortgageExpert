# VA Home Purchase Loan
Exclusively for Veterans, Active Duty Service Members, and eligible surviving spouses. 
- **Benefits**: No down payment required and no private mortgage insurance (PMI).
- **Funding Fee**: A one-time fee paid to the VA (can be rolled into the loan) unless the borrower has a service-connected disability.
