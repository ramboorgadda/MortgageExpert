# FHA Standard Loan (203b)
Insured by the Federal Housing Administration, this loan allows for lower credit scores and smaller down payments.
- **Requirement**: Minimum 580 FICO for 3.5% down; 500-579 requires 10% down.
- **MIP**: Mortgage Insurance Premium is required for the life of the loan if the down payment is less than 10%.
