# Closing Costs Breakdown
Expect to pay 2% to 5% of the home’s purchase price in closing costs.
- **Lender Fees**: Origination, underwriting, and processing fees.
- **Third-Party Fees**: Appraisal, title search, title insurance, and credit report fees.
- **Prepaids**: Initial escrow deposit for taxes and insurance.
