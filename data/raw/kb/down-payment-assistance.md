# Down Payment Assistance (DPA) Programs
DPA programs help first-time buyers cover upfront costs.
- **Grants**: Forgiven funds that do not need to be repaid.
- **Second Mortgages**: Low-interest or deferred-payment loans that cover the down payment.
- **Tax Credits**: Mortgage Credit Certificates (MCC) that provide a federal tax credit for a portion of the mortgage interest paid.
